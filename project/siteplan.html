Site Name

DTUP BO (De Todo Un Poco Bolivia)

The name DTUP BO stands for "De Todo Un Poco Bolivia," which translates to "A little bit of everything Bolivia." This name was chosen to reflect the diverse product range offered by the store, including cosmetics, PC accessories, and custom clothing (sublimado de ropa). The name is easy to remember, representative of the variety, and highlights that the store serves the Bolivian market.

Optional Domain Availability: dtup-bo.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the DTUP BO website is to provide an online platform for selling locally made products in Bolivia. The site will showcase a wide variety of products, including cosmetics, PC accessories, and custom sublimated clothing. Users will be able to browse the catalog, add items to their cart, and purchase directly through the site.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "What cosmetics are made with natural ingredients?"

    • The site will have detailed product descriptions, especially for cosmetics, to highlight natural ingredients and eco-friendly options.

  • Scenario 2: "How can I customize my clothing with sublimation?"

    • Customers will find information on how to place orders for custom sublimated clothing with step-by-step instructions on personalization.

  • Scenario 3: "I'm looking for a gift, what are the best options?"

    • The site will feature a 'Top Gifts' section where users can easily find gift-worthy products with special discounts.

Color Schema

The color scheme for DTUP BO will focus on modern, clean, and bold colors.

  • Teal (#004d40): Used for headings and navigation bars, giving a fresh and professional look.
  • White (#ffffff): Background color for the entire site, ensuring readability and a clean layout.
  • Orange (#ff7043): Accent color for buttons and call-to-actions to draw attention to interactive elements.

Typography

  • Font 1 (Headings): "Roboto", sans-serif - This modern and clean font will be used for headings and product titles.
  • Font 2 (Body Text): "Open Sans", sans-serif - This easy-to-read font will be used for the body content, ensuring clarity across all devices.
